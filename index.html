# Overview

Monte Carlo simulation is a forecast model that uses repeated random sampling to estimate the probability distribution of different outcomes.

# Get started

Test the default scenarios to understand how the system reacts to different conditions.

* In the sliders set your market assumptions, each run will forecast the stock price for the next 5 years.
* "Stock Delta" panel will display the total fluctuation for each run.

# Key features

* Identify market tendencies, after several runs, a "cloud" of projections will form on the chart, revealing a visual trend of probable price ranges. It helps to understand the best timing and amount to trade.
* Visualize stock volatility in response to market fluctuations: a stable stock will display a consistent dense line, one with higher beta will show an expanded cloud of possible values.
